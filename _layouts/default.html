CLEAN FINISH

With minimized yet striking animation, simplicity remains at the heart of everything that Anchovy does. Save time with the technologies you know and inherit our breathtaking yet manageable display.

Our emphasis on familiar technologies means that you don't have to learn an obscure Graphics Engine to continue development. Instead, a basic understanding of Java.AWT, Google Sketchup and Photoshop is all you need!

ACCESSIBLE CODE

Because minimizing change is our number one priority, you can be sure that if you knew Lamprey’s REACTOR, you’ll still know Anchovy’s REACTOR++. What’s changed in this development cycle? A single class.

To keep code as recognisable as possible, we simply removed TextUI, introduced a GUI Package and integrated the two systems together through a new, well commented SoftwareComponent class.

SUPERLATIVE SAFETY

Despite our minimal approach to development, we have gone above and beyond on testing so you can be confident in trusting our code.

Our Software Component class alone has more that 50 unit tests, more than doubling the amount that REACTOR was originally shipped with. Of course, we’ve also honoured all of the legacy tests we inherited, in addition to a myriad of new Dynamic and Acceptance tests as well!

INCREMENTAL DESIGN

REACTOR++ was named not only to symbolise the extension of REACTOR, but to emphasise our incremental development approach. By dividing the project into four smaller stages, work became simpler and easier to manage.

How does this benefit you? Because we’ve tested after each increment, our code is four times as safe. By breaking development into manageable increments, we minimised change and isolated hard to detect bugs so you don’t have to.

WHY ANCHOVY?

For Assessment 3 Team Anchovy Presents REACTOR++, an incremental extension of Team Lamprey's REACTOR. With an emphasis on minimal change, REACTOR++ is a safe, attractive and viable option for further development - perfect for teams with previous Lamprey experience. Our ethos is to modify our inherited systems as little as possible, so you can modify them to your hearts content with complete peace of mind.

As we have left the model and controller elements of REACTOR's original MVC completely untouched, REACTOR++ offers you the same development flexibility as we found it with. Easily extend the power plant's architecture with our pre-rendered 3D models of components for deceptively attractive and expansible displays. Or, take our origional Sketchup file, apply some textures and get creative! Further graphic development can be as simple or involved as you prefer.

We're also very aware that an unexpected change in requirements is the single largest cause of risk in software engineering. As all of the development teams will be exposed to this in Assessment 4, its important to choose well tested reliable code that you're familiar with. To help earn your well deserved trust, REACTOR++ has been extensively tested throughout development. With our agile approach breaking Assessment 3 into four manageable parts, Team Anchovy's REACTOR++ benefits from four times more regression tests than comparable teams using traditional development approaches. Furthermore, we've additionally invested in extensive Dynamic, Acceptance and Unit Testing for you're peace of mind, more than doubling the number of unique tests originally inherited.
